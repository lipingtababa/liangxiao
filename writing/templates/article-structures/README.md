# Article Structures (文章结构类型)

## Workflow

```
/brainstorm → /outline → /draft → /review → /convert
```

**Before writing, read:**
1. `PRINCIPLES.md` - 6 core principles (required)
2. Choose a structure below based on your topic
3. Read examples in that folder

---

## 5 Structures

## 1. Debunking (驳斥)

**Pattern**: X is widely believed → X is wrong → here's reality

**Examples**:
- `vibe-coding-critique.md` - Vibe Coding是专供程序员的成年人童话
- `infrastructure-department-value.md` - 基础架构部，还有必要吗？
- `multi-cloud-strategy.md` - 多云战略：无奈的现实，危险的选择

---

## 2. Raising a Valuable Question (提出问题)

**Pattern**: Reframe definition → challenge assumptions → open new perspective

**Examples**:
- `dba-not-database-user.md` - DBA不是数据库的用户，开发者才是！
- AI Coding领域的真问题 (not yet added)

---

## 3. Case and Product Study (案例与产品研究)

**Pattern**: Here's what happened → what it reveals → the lesson

**Examples**:
- `alibaba-cloud-security-incident.md` - 一个阿里云用户的故障
- `template-05-explorative-ai-use-case.md` - Template for explorative AI use cases

---

## 4. Exploration & Hypothesis (探索与假说)

**Pattern**: Here's a problem → my experiment/thinking → tentative framework, inviting feedback

**Examples**:
- `ai-coding-methodology.md` - AI Coding的真正问题不在沟通，而在方法论

---

## 5. Prediction/Trend Analysis (趋势预测)

**Pattern**: Current state → forces driving change → where it's heading

**Examples**:
- ChatGPT会怎么杀死Google/百度 (not yet added)
- Bing问答对Google的降维打击 (not yet added)
